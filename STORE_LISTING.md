## Full description
QuantumQR is a fast, modern QR code and barcode scanner with no ads and no
tracking. Point your camera and scan instantly — links, products, WiFi,
contacts and more.

Why QuantumQR:
- 100% ad-free — no banners, no popups, ever
- Private by design — no analytics, no account, works fully offline
- Minimal permissions — only the camera is required to scan
- Lightning fast — powered by CameraX and Google ML Kit
- Scans all major formats: QR, Data Matrix, Aztec, PDF417, UPC/EAN, Code 128, Code 39 and more
- Built-in QR generator — create and save QR codes to your gallery
- Batch mode — scan many codes in a row without stopping
- Scan history with CSV export
- Flashlight toggle for low light
- Opens links safely in your browser with an optional confirmation step

No sign-up. No subscription. Just a clean, reliable scanner that respects
your privacy.
